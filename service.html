Service

Giving back and serving the community is very important to me. Some ways that I have contributed to my community are...

  • Seton Northwest Volunteer
  • Central Texas Food Bank
  • Bryan Animal Center
All of these experiences have been for a great cause and motivate me to do many more opportunities such as these in the near future. My responsibilities at Seton Northwest were to assist in paperwork organizing, schedule appointments for physical therapy patients, as well as work in the giftshop. At Central Texas Food Bank, a pop-up truck containing various groceries are supplied to individuals in need. I aided the cause by volunteering at multiple of these pop-ups, handing out groceries to those who needed it. Lastly, I have recently started helping the Bryan Animal Center transport dogs to other larger shelters so they have a greater chance of getting adopted.
